Get Involved with Golgotha

Be part of something powerful. Golgotha is an independent feature film created by dedicated professionals who believe in bold storytelling and the enduring craft of cinema. Your support directly helps bring this vision to life — from production and post-production to festival release and distribution. Every contribution makes a difference, and every donor becomes part of the film’s legacy.

Ways to Contribute

$100 Credit Recognition

Minimum donation: $100

Your name will appear in the official end credits of Golgotha, a lasting acknowledgment of your support for independent filmmaking. Limited to 300–500 names for readability.

$10,000 Special Thanks Section

Minimum donation: $10,000

Reserved for only four donors. Each name appears in its own “Special Thanks” section, displayed in larger type and spaced apart for emphasis. A highly visible acknowledgment of your belief in the project.

High-Donor Packages (No Limit on Entrants)

Minimum donation: $5,000

All donors contributing $5,000 or more receive on-screen recognition, premiere screening tickets, and set-visit priority. The top three contributors by donation amount will receive special rewards:

  • 1st Place – $10,000 minimum: Dinner with the director, one producer, and one actor, plus an on-screen appearance.
  • 2nd Place – $7,500 minimum: Dinner with a producer and an appearance in the film.
  • 3rd Place – $5,000 minimum: Opportunity to appear in the movie.

Funniest Onscreen Credit Contest

Entry fee: $25

Submit your most creative or humorous credit name for a chance to appear in the final credits. The top three entries, judged on originality and humor, will be featured on screen.

Community and Outreach

Through partnerships, media appearances, and outreach by Mortenveld Productions, Golgotha is connecting with film lovers, creators, and supporters everywhere. Every share, mention, and contribution helps build the audience and energy behind this film.
