Funniest Onscreen Credit Contest

Official Rules and Disclaimer

Official Rules

  • Sponsor: Golgotha Films LLC.
  • Contest Type: Skill-based only. No element of chance is involved.
  • Eligibility: Open to individuals 18 years or older at the time of entry. Void where prohibited or restricted by law.
  • Entry Fee: $25 per entry. Entrants may submit as many entries as they wish.
  • Free Entry Option: To enter without payment, mail your name, contact information, and entry phrase to Golgotha Films LLC, 2088 Renault Ln NE, Atlanta GA, postmarked by the deadline.
  • Entry Requirements: Submit an original, creative, or humorous “credit name” suitable for public display. Entries must not contain profanity, hate speech, or any offensive content.
  • Submission Deadline: All entries must be received by December 1, 2025.
  • Judging Criteria:
    • Creativity (40%)
    • Humor and originality (40%)
    • Appropriateness for display (20%)
  • Judging Process: A panel of three representatives from Golgotha Films LLC will review all eligible entries based on the above criteria. Judges’ decisions are final.
  • Prizes: The top three entries will be featured in the end credits of Golgotha. The exact format, wording, and placement of the credit will be determined at the sole discretion of Golgotha Films LLC. No cash or alternative prizes will be offered.
  • Winner Notification: Winners will be notified by email the week of December 25, 2025.
  • Rights and Permissions: By entering, participants grant Golgotha Films LLC the right to use, modify, and display their submitted entry name in the film and related promotions without further payment or approval.
  • General Conditions: Golgotha Films LLC reserves the right to reject or remove any entry deemed inappropriate. All decisions are final. Void where prohibited.
