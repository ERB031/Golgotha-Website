Creative Collective

Profiles from the Golgotha company.

Seasoned collaborators from the horror, festival, and arthouse circuits steer Golgotha’s ritual with precision and empathy.

Director

Christopher Rosica

Christopher Rosica is a filmmaker whose work has premiered at Slamdance, Indie Memphis, and the New Orleans Film Festival. He was a finalist for the Museum of the Moving Image’s Sloan Science in Cinema grant and the Louisiana Film Prize, and his short It Was Kind of a Love Story (2024) advanced through the New Orleans Film Festival Pitch Forum and the Stowe Story Labs / NOFS Narrative Lab.

Screenwriter

Grace Caroline Curley

Grace Caroline Curley is an award-winning screenwriter and playwright based in New Orleans. Her short The Great Void (2024) premiered at FilmQuest, where it received Best Ensemble, and she has presented work with the Voodoo Mystery Symposium, the Tennessee Williams Literary Festival, and the Miskatonic Institute of Horror Studies.

Casting Director

Daniel Oakley

Daniel Oakley is a graduate of Circle in the Square Theatre School in New York City. He has coached performers alongside Philip Seymour Hoffman, Kevin Bacon, and Lady Gaga, and co-founded The Oakley Collective to unite actors and directors as collaborators.

Lead Producer

Ethan Beller

Ethan Beller is an Atlanta-based producer and frequent collaborator of Rosica’s. His work spans Vice, ESPN, AMC Networks, and a range of boutique features, pairing financing strategy with distribution and impact campaign expertise.

Associate Producer

Faith Nicholas

Faith Nicholas is a producer based in New York City. She has supported emerging horror voices across IFC Films, Broadway Video, and Screambox, and shares development insights with a community of more than 25,000 genre fans on TikTok.
