Director

Christopher Rosica

Christopher’s gothic-noir short Reliquary premiered at Fantasia International Film Festival before earning a midnight spotlight at Tribeca. His camera lore favours chiaroscuro tableaux and ritualistic blocking that fuse grief with genre spectacle.

Fantasia • Tribeca Midnight • Overlook FF

Screenwriter

Grace Caroline Curley

Grace’s script for The Waking Choir took home the genre jury award at Austin Film Festival and was workshopped through the Sundance Episodic Lab. Her pages braid Southern Gothic cadence with elevated horror mythologies.

Austin FF • Sundance Lab • Salem Horror

Lead Actor

Daniel Oakley

Daniel headlined the SXSW-winning thriller Mercy Blackout and earned a British Independent Film Award nomination for his turn in the folk-horror Sable Ash. He calibrates vulnerability and menace with uncanny precision.

SXSW • BIFA • Sitges

Lead Producer

Ethan Beller

Ethan produced the Tribeca breakout Gods of Dust and shepherded the Slamdance winner Night Vespers, developing a holistic financing roadmap that keeps boutique horror artist-driven and investor-ready.

Tribeca • Slamdance • Fantasia

Casting Director

Faith Nicholas

Faith is a New York casting producer whose curated slates powered the Brooklyn Horror Film Festival audience award winner Doctrine and the Overlook selection The Archivist. She champions boundary-pushing genre talent with digital-first reach.

Brooklyn Horror • Overlook • SeriesFest
